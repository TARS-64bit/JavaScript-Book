Instructions

How to search a page with a page number?

Enter the page number in the white box at the top of your screen and press enter.

Currently the white box is showing 1 /10, enter 2 and press enter key to go to the next page. If you wish to navigate to the Cover page just type cover in the white box or type index to visit index page.

Add Favourate Page To Favourate Page List

To add a page to your Favourate Page List just click on the heart symbol at the top of the page title. On this page you can find the heart symbol on top of the title "Instructions", click on it and this page will be added to your favourate's list. To remove the page from the favourate's list click again on the heart symbol. To remove all the pages from the favourate's list click on the Clear button at the bottom of the list.

Navigate using Next and Previous Buttons

You can find Next and Previous buttons on each page and on the left and right side of the page number at the top. Next button takes you to the next page and previous button takes you to the previous page.
